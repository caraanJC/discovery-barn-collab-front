About Us

Looking for inspiration to incorporate plants into your homes? Or maybe, you just want to share those variegated monsteras you grew from seeds? Greenspace is the place to be. A hub for plant enthusiasts to share their love for plants while creating beautiful spaces. Together, let us make those bare corners greener than ever!

Importance of Green Spaces

More than the aesthetics, plants are generally good for our health. They help improve air quality by eliminating air pollutants, relieve stress, boost productivity and creativity, and promote faster recovery from illnesses. They're an inexpensive way to add instant color and glamour to home interiors.
